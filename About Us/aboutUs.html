About Us

Here at Liv-stock, we believe that every voice matters and every single step counts. And we wish to put that belief into practice. The common sight of cattle wandering on roads and feeding on garbage bits broke our hearts every single time. The plight of the sacred animal in a country that worships them was our motivation toward creating a platform to make a difference. We, at Liv-stock, aim at raising funds for all the cow shelter establishments for the betterment of upkeep and nourishment of the cattle and providing them with health monitoring systems for regular health check-ups as well, aiming at overall well-being of the sacred beast. We are inspired to change the living conditions of the cattle country-wide and bring them out of their misery. And the team of Liv-stock is collectively calling out to you to come join hands with us in our journey of turning this vision into a reality.
